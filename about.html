About STEPHANUS MINISTRY

STEPHANUS MINISTRY is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law and operates for charitable purposes as a recognized 501(c)(3) with EIN 86-2682518.

Specific purpose: provide services to other organizations — strengthening nonprofits, ministries, and community groups through training, shared logistics, and resource coordination.

  • No substantial part of our activities involves lobbying or political campaigning.
  • Assets are irrevocably dedicated to charitable and educational purposes.
  • Upon dissolution, remaining assets go to a similar 501(c)(3).
